# Guru Sishya — Product Hunt Launch Plan

## Why Product Hunt?
- Free, gets 500-5,000 visitors in 24 hours
- Backlinks from high-authority domain (DA 90+)
- Tech-savvy audience = your exact target market
- Social proof for future marketing

---

## Pre-Launch Checklist (Do 1 Week Before)

### 1. Create Product Hunt Account
- Sign up at [producthunt.com](https://www.producthunt.com)
- Complete your profile (photo, bio, links)
- Start engaging: upvote 5-10 products, leave 3-5 comments
- You need some activity before launching (PH penalizes brand new accounts)

### 2. Get a Hunter
- Ideally someone with 500+ followers hunts your product
- Ask in: PH community, Twitter, LinkedIn
- OR self-hunt (works fine for first launch)

### 3. Prepare Assets
- **Thumbnail**: 240x240px, your flame+book logo
- **Gallery images** (5 max, 1270x760px each):
  1. Hero shot: landing page screenshot
  2. Topic hub with 6 feature cards (Guru's Path, Pariksha, etc.)
  3. Quiz/Pariksha in action
  4. Analytics dashboard
  5. Company prep paths (Google/Amazon/Meta)
- **Maker video** (optional, 1-2 min): screen recording of using the app

## Best Day to Launch
- **Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday** — highest engagement
- **Avoid Monday** (too competitive) and **Friday** (low engagement)
- **Avoid weekends**
