## What Works in 2026

- 8-12 min videos (optimal for new channels)
- Curiosity gap titles ("What Happens When...")
- Series format (binge behavior = algorithm love)
- Comment engagement within 60 minutes
- Consistent niche (interview prep, not general programming)
- Platform CTA in every video (guru-sishya.in)

## What's Dead in 2026

- SEO keyword stuffing (AI reads video content now)
- "Subscribe and hit the bell" begging
- Upload frequency over quality
- Repurposing long-form clips as Shorts (make Shorts natively)
- Generic "Python Tutorial for Beginners" without unique angle
